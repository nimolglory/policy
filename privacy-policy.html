1. Overview

Khmer Calendar is a traditional Khmer lunar calendar app. We built it to be simple, private, and offline-first. This policy explains exactly what data we access, why, and how it is handled.

Short version: We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. All your settings are stored locally on your device. The only optional data you ever send us is the feedback message you choose to write.

2. Permissions & Why We Need Them

Permission Why it is needed
INTERNET Required for three optional features: sending feedback via Telegram, fetching live gold prices (Yahoo Finance), and fetching exchange rates (National Bank of Cambodia). The app works fully offline without these features.
READ_CALENDAR Reads events from your device calendar so they can be displayed alongside Khmer calendar dates. This data never leaves your device.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Sends local on-device reminders for Sila days, public holidays, festivals, and the daily horoscope. Notifications are generated entirely on your device — no server is involved.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Re-schedules your notification alarms after the device is restarted so you continue receiving reminders.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally only using Android SharedPreferences and never transmitted:

  • Theme and language preferences
  • Notification on/off toggles and reminder time
  • Cached gold price and exchange rate (to reduce repeat network calls)
  • App settings (show Rasei, show device calendar, etc.)

4. Network Requests

Feature Destination Data Sent Personal?
Feedback form api.telegram.org Name (optional), contact (optional), message text Optional
Gold price query1.finance.yahoo.com None — read-only public data request None
Exchange rate data.mef.gov.kh (National Bank of Cambodia) None — read-only public data request None

No IP address, device identifier, or personal information is included in the gold price or exchange rate requests.

5. Feedback Data

The in-app feedback form is entirely optional. If you use it:

  • Name and contact fields are optional — you may leave them blank.
  • Your message is sent directly to the developer via Telegram Bot API over HTTPS.
  • This data is used only to respond to your feedback. It is not stored in any database, not sold, and not shared with any third party.
  • To request deletion of a submitted message, email nimolxyz@gmail.com.

6. Device Calendar Access

With your permission, the app reads events from your device's calendar to display them alongside Khmer dates. This data is read locally and is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

7. Advertising & Analytics

No Ads No Advertising ID No Analytics SDK No Crash Reporting No Tracking

This app contains no advertisements and does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting libraries.

8. Children's Privacy

Khmer Calendar is a general-purpose calendar app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Security

All network communication (feedback, gold price, exchange rate) is performed over HTTPS. No sensitive personal data is collected or stored on our servers.

10. Your Rights

  • Access / Deletion: Since we store no personal data on our servers, there is nothing to access or delete beyond what is stored locally on your device. You can clear all app data at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Khmer Calendar → Clear Data.
  • Feedback deletion: Email nimolxyz@gmail.com to request removal of any feedback message you submitted.
  • Permissions: You can revoke any permission (Calendar, Notifications) at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Khmer Calendar → Permissions.
